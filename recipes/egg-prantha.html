Ingridients

  • Chapati Dough
  • Capsicum
  • Green Beans
  • Green Chillies
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps to make Egg Prantha

  1. Take flour, ajwain, salt in a bowl. Add water and knead it to a soft dough..Cover and let it rest for 30 mins.
  2. Now mix all the ingredients for filling.
  3. Take a small portion of dough out. Roll it into circle, drizzle oil and sprinkle with dry flour. Fold in half, drizzle oil and sprinkle with dry flour. Now fold in triangle and roll it out.
  4. Place paratha on hot tawa and cook for few seconds on one side, flip over and cook for few seconds. Now remove the top layer of the paratha gently and spoon some filling in. Flip over carefully and cook till done.
  5. Drizzle oil or ghee over it and cook till golden on both sides.
  6. Serve hot.
